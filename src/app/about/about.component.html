Wage Disseminator

Have you come across the time where you're not aware of how much money you end up spending for the month & you decide that you need a "Plan" next time..?

Honestly, I don't care :)

This application is created to eliminate the confusion of how much money you're supposed to spend categorically.

Note: This application is based on local storage and I can assure you that no data is stolen from you.

This is an Open Source Angular project and interested people can fork it or Contribute. Thanks :D (I'm pretty sure you're lazy enough to do neither of em)
