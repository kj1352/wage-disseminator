Quick Tips
  1. To get the best practical experience of this app, having multiple bank account is preferable.
  2. Clear one of your accounts to make it a 0 balance.
  3. When your salary comes in, refer the app to know how much money you're supposed to spend / transfer / save / donate for the month and do your deeds.
  4. There is a psychological aspect involved behind this App.
  5. Since you are aware of the exact amount you can spend for the month, your mind will passively keep track of your expenditure and you will know it before you hit the expense limit.
